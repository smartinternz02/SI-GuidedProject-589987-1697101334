Envisioning Success: Predicting University Scores With Machine Learning

The project describes the importance of scoring universities and highlights the benefits of such a system. It mentions that university scores can help prospective students make informed decisions, provide universities with feedback for improvement, and impact funding and reputation. The project described involves a dataset with university characteristics, with the target variable being the university's "Score," which is predicted based on parameters like quality of education, alumni employment, faculty quality, publications, influence, citations, and patents. The goal of the project is to create a web application for predicting university scores to assist students in making educational choices.
